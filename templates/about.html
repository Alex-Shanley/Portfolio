MY BACKGROUND

I started my journey into programming in a non-traditional way. I graduated from secondary school in 2016 and went straight into retail. I had been working part-time as a cleaner since I was 16, and by the age of 19, I worked my way up to a manager position, which I held for four years. I earned my first degree in Animation from Griffith College, then completed a second degree in Graphic Design, where I transitioned into UX/UI design.

I then landed my first job as a designer and gradually worked my way up to project manager, a title I currently hold till this day.

One of my favourite quotes that really inspired me on my journey is: "Those who say they can, and those who say they can't, are usually both right."

ABOUT ME

I've always had a deep love for music both the classics and the new. Growing up with a father in a band, I have vivid memories of him taking me to concerts across Ireland, and it was there that I truly fell in love with music in all its forms. As I got older, I knew I wanted to share that same passion with others. That's how Headline was born — a place where people can discover new jams and classic hits, all in one spot, just as I experienced it.

Headline was my way of giving back to the world what music gave to me. It’s a platform where everyone can explore the sounds that have shaped my life whether it’s uncovering fresh tracks or revisiting timeless classics.
